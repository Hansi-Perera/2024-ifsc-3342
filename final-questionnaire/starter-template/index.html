Site Planning Questionnaire

Who is the target audience?

My target audience will be Sri Lankan natives as well as tourists and possible donors.

How can I tailor the website to reach that audience?

I can pay attention to the theme and keep it similar while making it more professional with better accessibility for a range of devices. I also will attempt to structure it better instead of the given cheap/ free template started website that exists.

What are the goals for the site?

The site is dedicated to wildlife conservation and sustainable development. The site educates people on different projects that support their main goal and gives an opportunity for people to donate.

How will I gather the information?

Mostly from the created website. I plan to add a few extra things and change the layout somewhat.

What are my sources for multimedia content?

There are pictures and videos on the website.

What is my budget?

Pretty much nothing, I am just working to recreate this website with responsiveness.

How long do I have to complete the project?

A few weeks

Who is on my project team?

Myself at firrt but I will be taking advice from peers, teachers and family.

How often should the site be updated?

Every few weeks.

Who is responsible for updating the site?

Since it is a government site, technically it would be their government as new projects come and information on the population and wildlife changes.
